Strawberry Ice Cream

By Rida Aftab

Ingredients

  • Strawberry 250 grams
  • Cream 1 packet
  • Condensed milk 1 tin
  • Gelatin powder 1 tsp
  • Milk 2 cups
  • Water 4 tbsp

Cooking Directions

  1. First put the gelatin powder in water.
  2. Now add strawberry, cream, condensed milk, gelatin, milk, water in a blender.
  3. Then put it into air tight box and freeze it.
